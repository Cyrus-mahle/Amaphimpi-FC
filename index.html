VISION and MISSION STATEMENT

  • VISION
    Amaphimpi Football Club envisions a vibrant future, uniting the community of Ntabankulu, Eastern Cape, through the beautiful game of football. Committed to fostering excellence, inclusivity, and integrity, we empower players to thrive on and off the field. Together, we embody the values of sportsmanship and local pride, creating a lasting legacy of passion, camaraderie, and athletic achievement, shaping a brighter tomorrow for generations to come, igniting hope and unity throughout our beloved region.
  • MISSION
    Amaphimpi Football Club's mission is to unite Eastern Cape's best players, particularly from Ntabankulu, fostering talent development and teamwork. Our goal is not only to excel on the field but also to contribute to our community's well-being. By providing a positive outlet for youth, we aim to decrease crime in Ntabankulu, creating a brighter future for South Africa. Through football, we instill values of discipline, resilience, and leadership, empowering our players to become role models and agents of positive change in their communities.
